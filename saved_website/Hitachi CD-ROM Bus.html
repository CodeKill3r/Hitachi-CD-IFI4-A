CD-ROM Bus vs. SCSI

In order to maintain or repair these old drives, here are the SCSI counterparts listed:
  • CDR 1553S (1503S)
  • CDR 3650 (3600 + 64kB cache)
  • CDR 1650S (1600S)
  • CDR 1750S (1700S)
  • CDR 3750 SCSI-II (3700)

Drivers

For DOS and OS/2
Download 1 (Germany): hitachi.zip
Download 2 (Australia): hitachi.zip

Adapter Description File (ADF) for MCA: @5eee.adf

DOS drivers

HITACHIA.SYS - ISA/EISA adapter cards
HITACHIB.SYS - MCA adapter card

CONFIG.SYS:
DEVICE=x:\path\HITACHIA.SYS /D:name /N:1 /P:300
AUTOEXEC.BAT:
MSCDEX.EXE /D:name /M:8

OS/2 drivers

HITCDISA.ADD - ISA/EISA adapter cards
HITCDMC.ADD - MCA adapter card

CONFIG.SYS:
BASEDEV=HITCDISA.ADD /P:300
